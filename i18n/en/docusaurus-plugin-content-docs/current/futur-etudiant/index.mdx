## The New Insaiën's TODO List!
- [ ] Complete your **administrative registration**: You can do it online on the UPHF website (INSA HdF is a part of UPHF): [inscription.uphf.fr](https://inscription.uphf.fr)
- [ ] **Create your UPHF account**: You will receive an email with instructions.
- [ ] **Download the UPHF app**: Find your schedule, emails, grades, and all the latest updates.
- [ ] **Join the INSA HdF Discord**: Connect with your future classmates.
- [ ] **Follow the Instagram accounts of the BDE and INSA**: [@bdeinsahdf](https://instagram.com/bdeinsahdf) and [@insahdf](https://instagram.com/insahdf) to stay informed – including about the integration week!
- [ ] **Prepare for your arrival**: Check out guides on housing, transportation, and the campus.
- [ ] **Participate in the integration week**: Don’t miss the welcome events to meet new people.
- [ ] **Discover the associations**: Explore clubs and activities to get involved from the start.
- [ ] **Get to know the school’s services**: Library, health, sports, etc.

## Who are we?

We are the new BDE of INSA Hauts-de-France, from the **CampINSA** list, elected in May 2024 for the **2025-2026 term**, after a memorable campaign against the Carrivederci list.

Our mission? **To make your time at INSA an unforgettable experience** by organizing events, representing students, and fostering cohesion between all classes.

### Our commitment:

- Offer **festive, cultural, and sports events** throughout the year
- **Defend students' interests** with the administration
- **Energize campus life** with parties, weekends, and unique activities
- Encourage **integration and mutual support** among students of all years

Whether you're new to INSA or a regular in student life, the BDE is here to support you and help you **live an exceptional student adventure**. Join us and be part of this great adventure with us!

[Follow us on our social networks to not miss any upcoming events](https://linktr.ee/bdeinsahdf)!

## Who are we?

We are the new BDE of INSA Hauts-de-France, from the **CAMP'INSA** list, elected in May 2024 for the **2025-2026 term**, after a memorable campaign against the Carrivederci list.

Our mission? **Make your time at INSA an unforgettable experience**, by organizing events, representing students, and fostering cohesion among all classes.

### Our commitment:

- Offering **festive, cultural, and sports events** throughout the year
- **Defending students' interests** with the administration
- **Energizing campus life** with parties, weekends, and unique activities
- Encouraging **integration and mutual support** among students of all years

Whether you're new to INSA or a veteran of student life, the BDE is here to support you and help you **live an exceptional student adventure**. Join us and be part of this great journey with us!

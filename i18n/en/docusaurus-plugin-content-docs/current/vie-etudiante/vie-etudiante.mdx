## Associations
The school places great importance on student involvement in associative life. To allow students to engage, **no classes are held on Thursday afternoons**. So, it's time for associations, sports, culture, and good times!

INSA Hauts-de-France is full of student associations to appeal to everyone. Whether you're sporty, artistic, geeky, party-loving, committed, or all of the above, you'll definitely find something that suits you!

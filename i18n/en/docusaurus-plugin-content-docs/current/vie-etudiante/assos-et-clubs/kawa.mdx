## The Objective of Kawa

The objective of Kawa is to provide cold and hot beverages at low prices to all students.

### Menu

**Hot Beverages**  
- Coffee: €0.5  
- Tea: €0.4  
- Hot Chocolate: €0.6  
- Cappuccino/Mocha: €0.7  

**Cold Beverages**  
- Cold Drinks: €0.8 per can  

**Snacks**  
- Sweet Bars: €0.8 per bar  
- Waffle (sugar/chocolate): €0.7  

**Meals**  
- Pizza: €1.6  
- Instant Noodles: €0.8

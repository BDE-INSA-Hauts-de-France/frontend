## Welcome, fellow enthusiasts of the gaming world!

Our Club's main goal is to **have fun together around board games**, whether it's with simple, fun, and party games, abstract games like chess, or strategic board games, some of which can last for hours!

No matter the game you want to play, you'll find people to enjoy it with, or you'll discover new games that will let you explore the richness of this gaming universe.

We also aim to **create our own game** to present it at the **Ludinord festival**.

**You can also borrow games** if you'd like. Feel free to contact us on **Insta**!

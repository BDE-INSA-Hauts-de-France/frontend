### **We are Valmev!** The mechanical association of your school.

Our goal is simple: to design a single-seater from scratch to participate in the **Shell Eco Marathon**!

The Shell Eco Marathon is a competition where the objective is to travel the most kilometers with only **1 liter of fuel**!

So, if you love mechanics, whether you're a novice or experienced, **don't hesitate and join us**!

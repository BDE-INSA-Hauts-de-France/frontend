## ./insa.sh - Club Info at INSA HdF! 🎉

Our mission? To bring together beginners, curious individuals, and tech enthusiasts through projects, conferences, and events, while providing help to those in need. 💻✨

We participate in various events, including:
- **The European INCYBER Conference** 🔐
- **La Nuit de l’info (Night of Info)** 🌙
- **The Square Wars**: our very own PixelWar at INSA HdF! 🕹️

This year, we also developed our brand new website. 🌐

No expertise required to join us – we warmly welcome all curious minds! 😊

**Passionate about technology or simply curious?** Want to learn more and organize tech-related events? Drop by on a Thursday afternoon! 🗓️

**PS:** And if you need help with a lab project, are having computer troubles, or need assistance with anything else, we're here for you! 🛠️💡
